Her Name Is Beauty

Her Name Is Beauty

Many wonders of the world had struck me with awe.
I felt proud of myself thinking I'd beheld it all,
But you appeared and I realized how wrong I'd been
For you possessed the kind of splendour I had never seen,
I was speechless for there was no word worthy to describe you
and your beauty, till now my eyes cannot get used to.

You look beautiful even without it being your intention.
And aphrodite herself would look at you with a bit of detestation
For your radiant skin is something only Goddesses could boast about.
That's why many hear of it and they can't help but doubt.

Whenever you look me in the eye, my face turns red.
And it's hard controlling some thoughts that run in my head
Those that belong in the deep hours of the night
For I felt your physique couldn't only be appreciated by sight.

Your voice has a pleasing effect on the most troubled hearts
And the melodies you produce are lovely works of art.
Whoever calls you theirs knows the true definition of satisfaction
For if you were mine, no one else would have my heart and attention.

A Lovely Fantasy

A Lovely Fantasy

I stayed in my dream for so long it felt like eternity
It was an unpleasant journey returning to reality
I`d blinded myself with my own feelings for you
But when the love mist cleared, everything began to look new.

I admit to creating that fantasy all my own
I'd grown weary of spending so much time alone
So when with beautiful strides you approached me
My eye said a being who loves me is what they see.
I believed those words and in it I decided to live
Because to me the real world had nothing better to give
I was comfortable living in this bright darkness
For in it I felt loved, the kind that rid me of my sadness.
So in the air I proudly built the castles we were to dwell
Hoping they'd materialize so I'd never return to reality; this hell
But you were the first to excuse yourself from our perfect team
And I was left to find my way home: to wake up from my dream.

The green grass we danced on barefooted in the rain
In reality, are just burning coals I walked on feeling nothing but pain.
For through the hurt you caused me by your departure may be unreal,
As my feet are burned, I can brag of one emotion in or relationship being real.

His Peace

His Peace

I was lost and deep inside me i was broken
When I laid down to sleep, I'd wish I wouldn't awaken
For the pain could become so unbearable
That my mind grew weak and I was unable
To explain the things I really felt within,
And I had nothing and no purpose to believe in.

It's sad to say this wasn't always how it was
I wish I knew this was what my actions would cause
For I had drowned myself in the world's pleasures
Striving to possess all of its treasures.
But the more I obtained the stronger the hunger grew
Soon my very self and dignity, away I eventually threw.
For I thought they were all worth my happiness
All I did was create and strengthen my own emptiness.
And when there was nothing else in the world to claim
My life became worthless and flooded with shame.

In that miserable state, someone still loved me.
He stood in front of me and I felt a sudden urge to plead.
For I knew my actions had hurt him more
Yet he said he still loves me just like he did before.
In my darkest moments this man came as light,
And told me these battles I'd no longer fight.
He picked me up and gave me his peace
And he put my broken heart together piece by piece.

When An Artist Falls

When An Artist Falls

There was once an artist,
He was just a boy; a shy lad in distress
Who at the sight of a being without flaw
Was redeemed, but willingly trapped himself in a prison, One he proudly created and called love.

With elegance this being came by.
And though words she uttered none,
Her smile spoke the prettiest language,
Her eyes conversed secretly with his
And every graceful step she took
Boast humbly of her glorious queenship
To all who stared and stood there speechless.

The artist knew a painting by the best would not justify her.
Her beauty could not be expressed on any canvas
For that wonderful soul of hers would lack recognition.
And that was what he treasured most about her.

So he discarded his brush and painter her with words,
Intending to bring out more than her curves
And hoping that at her exposure to this masterpiece
She'd understand how this artist truly felt.

Heartstrings

Heartstrings

I played those chords on my guitar for you,
Praying you'd look my way even once.
I'd lost count of the notes I'd mixed up looking at you
Yet I still created sweet melodies which attracted many,
I just wanted you to realize that I was here for you.

My piano seems to know exactly your favourite song,
For as I sat and saw you, I knew exactly what to play.
The key I needed came out of nowhere but it was as perfect as you.
You smiled as you heard the song and finally look my way.

When you started walking towards me I heard drum rolls,
And as you continued smiling cymbals joined in,
They were loud at first but became softer as you got closer.
And when you spoke they ended with a deep sound from a bass drum.
That sound was made by my heart, it got too excited by your voice.

Music had brought us together and love had binded us.
Now you say there's one song of mine you cherish most.
It wasn't the one from my saxophone that always made you smile as I played
Nor was it the notes from the valves of my trumpet that soothed you smiled as I played
You'd fallen in love with the music you heard when your head was on my chest
A song written specially for you and played with heartstrings.

Heartless

Heartless

You looked straight into my eyes,
And you told me when you leave I'll never hurt again.
I wanted to believe it was a lie
Like everything else you kept saying to me.
So I kept staring into those beautiful eyes of yours
Trying to decipher what they truly kept hidden in them.
For I desperately needed to convince my mind even a little
That my heart made no mistake in letting you close.

When finally felt your hand on my heart,
It was hard to believe that it had to come to this.
And when you held it and ripped it out of my chest,
Everything you'd said and done became clear.
And from there I was never hurt again like you said.
For you made me exactly like you, heartless.
